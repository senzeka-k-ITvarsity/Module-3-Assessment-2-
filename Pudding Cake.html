Pudding Cake

  1. The pudding cake is aptly named as it is a bit of both desserts. Its dual personality gives us a clue to its origin. Puddings, steamed or boiled, have always had a valued place at the American table, and early settlers experimented with native berries and fruits, maple syrup and molasses as flavorings.
